Chana Masala (Indian Chickpea Curry)

Description

Give this chana masala dish a try and don't look back! This Indian chickpea curry features a hearty mixture of chickpeas, tomatoes, onion, green chili, and warm Indian spices. Both carnivores and vegetarians will enjoy this meal. My kids love it. Serve over basmati or jasmine rice and enjoy. Namaste y'all!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 green chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional, adjust to taste)
  • 2 (15-ounce) cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 (14.5-ounce) cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, garam masala, and red chili powder. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the spices are fragrant.
  4. Add the drained chickpeas, diced tomatoes (with their juice), and water. Stir well to combine.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Stir occasionally.
  6. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro leaves before serving.
  8. Serve hot with basmati or jasmine rice, or with naan bread.
