Paneer Tikka Masala

Description:

This paneer tikka masala made with cubes of paneer cheese in a spicy creamy curry sauce is easy to make. This vegetarian dish goes well with naan or basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 pounds paneer, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • 4 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium green bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 medium jalapeño peppers, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ground cashews
  • 2 teaspoons garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons ginger paste
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 2 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 pints half-and-half
  • 2 teaspoons salt, or to taste

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add paneer cubes; cook and stir until golden, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add onions, bell pepper, jalapeños, ground cashews, garlic paste, ginger paste, cayenne pepper, cumin, coriander, and garam masala; cook and stir until well combined and fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Mix tomato sauce, half-and-half, and salt into paneer mixture; simmer until thickened, about 30 minutes.
  5. Return paneer to the skillet and stir to coat with sauce. Cook until heated through, about 5 minutes. Serve hot.
